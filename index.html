Instructions to Candidates

  1. Admit Card & ID Proof: Bring this Admit Card and a valid photo ID (Aadhaar or EPIC) to enter the examination hall.
  2. Punctuality: Entry will not be allowed if you are more than 10 minutes late after the exam begins.
  3. Duplicate Admit Card: Report loss of Admit Card to the Head of Department. A duplicate will be issued with a fee of â‚¹50.
  4. Leaving the Hall: You are not allowed to leave the hall before the exam ends.
  5. Items Not Allowed: Do not bring books, notes, loose sheets, mobile phones, smart devices and glasses, calculators, or any such items.
  6. Use of Scribe: Only eligible candidates (e.g., visually impaired) may use a scribe. Scribes will be provided by the Department. Inform the Centre Supervisor at least one week in advance. Costs are to be borne by the candidate.
  7. Writing Instructions: Use only blue or black ballpoint pens. Pencils may be used only for diagrams or maps.
  8. Filling Details: Fill in all required details on your answer sheet accurately. Incomplete or incorrect details may lead to disqualification.
  9. Attempting Questions: Follow all instructions on the question paper. Extra questions attempted beyond the allowed number will not be checked.
  10. Numbering Answers: Number your answers correctly. Misnumbered answers will not be checked.
  11. Requesting Marks: Submit your original or attested Admit Card when applying for marks or results.
  12. Unfair Means: Cheating, talking, or bringing banned items will result in immediate disqualification and a ban from future exams.
  13. Permission to Leave: You must get permission from the Invigilator before leaving the exam hall.
