Empowering Construction Workers

We provide financial support, education, and skill development to uplift the lives of construction workers and their families in Mizoram.

Our Services

Maternity Benefits

Female members receive ₹10,000 for up to two children to support their maternity needs.

Pension Support

Members aged 60+ get ₹1,000 monthly pension. Disabled members may receive up to ₹1,00,000.

Death Benefits

Up to ₹3,00,000 for workplace accident deaths, ₹1,00,000 for other causes, and ₹5,000 for funeral expenses.

Marriage Assistance

₹10,000 for the first marriage of members or their two children after 3 years of membership.

Medical Support

Up to ₹50,000/year for hospitalization or ₹1,00,000 one-time for chronic illnesses like cancer.

Educational Aid

Up to ₹20,000/year for children’s education, with ₹50,000 for top academic performers.

Skill Development

Access to training programs for members and their children to enhance skills and job opportunities.
